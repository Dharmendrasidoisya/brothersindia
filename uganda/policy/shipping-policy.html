Shipping Policy

We ship exclusively within India. Products ordered through our website for delivery within India are shipped via our courier partners, provided the delivery service is available to the destination. If courier service is unavailable at the desired location, the products will be shipped via the Indian Postal Service (Speed Post).

Shipping Time

Standard shipping typically takes up to 7 working days for orders within India, though delivery may be faster in many cases. If your order does not arrive on time, please contact us at info@brothersindia.com.

Responsibility and Transit Times

Brothers Pharmamatch (India) Pvt.Ltd. is not responsible for service transit times. Delivery times are subject to factors beyond our control, such as weather conditions, strikes, or other unforeseen events (“Force Majeure Event”). Transit times provided by our courier services may vary, especially during peak periods.

Shipping Costs

We Offer Free Shipping across india for all your order. For any questions regarding shipping, please email us at info@brothersindia.com. Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email notification with the tracking number.
