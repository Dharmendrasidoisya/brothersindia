PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Brothers India is a leading manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of Automatic Capsule Filling Machine in India, we specialize in delivering high-performance solutions tailored for pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries. Our Automatic Capsule Filling and Closing Machines are engineered to meet diverse production demands, offering models with output capacities of up to 90,000 and 40,000 capsules per hour. Designed for versatility, each unit supports powder, pellet, and tablet filling through dedicated change parts, ensuring seamless adaptation to varied formulations and batch requirements.

Built for precision and reliability, these machines incorporate a range of advanced built-in features along with optional enhancements to optimize operational efficiency, reduce downtime, and maintain consistent fill accuracy. Whether for domestic deployment or global export, our capsule filling systems combine robust engineering with user-friendly controls, making them ideal for manufacturers seeking scalable, GMP-compliant automation.
